Costs

Translation costs are determined in one of two ways. If your document is less than 10 pages, the cost will be $25.00-30.00 per hour. If your document is 10 or more pages, then the cost will be $25.00-30.00 per page. I provide a range because I also need to take document complexity into account when determining the final price. When you submit your original documentation, I will provide you with an approximate timeframe so that you can determine if that that will work before the final commitment is made for the work to be done.
